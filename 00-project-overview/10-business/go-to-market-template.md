# Go‑To‑Market Plan

## ICP & Positioning

**Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)**:

**Primary ICP: Engaged Parents (Germany & Austria)**
- **Demographics**: Parents aged 28-42 with children aged 2-10
- **Location**: Germany (primary), Austria (secondary)
- **Income**: €60k-150k household income
- **Pain**: Want quality bonding time, encourage reading, create lasting memories
- **Trigger**: Child's birthday, holiday gifting, starting school, screen time concerns
- **Budget**: €35 per book, €60/year subscription
- **Decision maker**: Mother (70%), father (20%), joint (10%)
- **Tech savvy**: Comfortable with apps, online shopping, AI tools
- **Timeline**: Immediate (impulse buy) to 1-2 weeks (gift planning)

**Secondary ICP: Grandparents (Gift Buyers)**
- **Demographics**: Grandparents aged 55-75 with grandchildren aged 2-10
- **Location**: Germany, Austria (local to grandchildren)
- **Income**: Comfortable pension or retired professionals
- **Pain**: Want meaningful gifts, not more toys, stay connected with grandchildren
- **Trigger**: Birthdays, holidays (Christmas, Easter), special occasions
- **Budget**: €35-50 per book (less price-sensitive)
- **Decision maker**: Grandmother (60%), grandfather (15%), joint (25%)
- **Tech savvy**: Moderate (uses smartphone, online shopping)
- **Timeline**: 1-4 weeks (planned gifting)

**Tertiary ICP: Israeli Hebrew-Speaking Parents**
- **Demographics**: Parents aged 25-40 with children aged 2-10
- **Location**: Israel (Tel Aviv, Jerusalem, Haifa)
- **Income**: ₪300k-700k household income (~€75k-175k)
- **Pain**: Limited Hebrew children's content, want to preserve language and culture
- **Trigger**: Child's birthday, Jewish holidays, Hebrew language development
- **Budget**: ₪120-150 per book (~€30-35)
- **Decision maker**: Mother (65%), father (25%), joint (10%)
- **Tech savvy**: Very high (tech-savvy population)
- **Timeline**: Immediate to 2 weeks

**Positioning Statement**:
For parents who want to create magical bonding moments with their children, KidBook Creator is the AI-powered storybook platform that lets families create personalized, professionally illustrated children's books in minutes and receive them as beautiful printed keepsakes; unlike generic books or expensive template-based services (€30-40), we offer unlimited AI-generated creativity with your child as the hero, starting with one free book.

## Launch Plan

**Pre-Launch (Weeks -8 to -1)**:

**Week -8 to -6: Foundation**
- Finalize brand identity (logo, colors, child-friendly design)
- Website development (German primary, Hebrew/English versions)
- Set up analytics (Mixpanel for user behavior, RevenueCat for revenue)
- Create sample books (5-7 high-quality examples)
- Set up email marketing (ConvertKit or Mailchimp)
- Test AI story generation and content moderation
- Test print quality with 3-5 sample books

**Week -5 to -3: Content & Beta Testing**
- Beta program: 20-30 parents create free books
- Collect feedback on UX, story quality, print quality
- Create "How it works" video (60 seconds)
- Write 3-5 blog posts (parenting tips, literacy development)
- Set up Instagram and Facebook pages
- Reach out to 10 parenting micro-influencers
- Create ad creatives (carousel, video, stories)

**Week -2 to -1: Soft Launch**
- Soft launch website (live but not promoted)
- Test full funnel: free book → registration → print order
- Test payment processing (Stripe, PayPal)
- Set up email automation (welcome series, nurture)
- Finalize print partner agreements
- Prepare launch announcement (Instagram, Facebook, email)
- Set up referral program

**Launch (Week 0)**:

**Day 1-3: Public Launch (Germany/Austria)**
- Instagram/Facebook announcement with sample books
- Email to beta users and warm network
- Post in German parenting Facebook groups (5-10 groups)
- Activate influencer partnerships (5-10 influencers post)
- Press release to German parenting media
- Reddit post in r/Eltern (authentic, not spammy)

**Week 1-2: Initial Traction**
- Daily Instagram posts/stories (user-generated content)
- Respond to all inquiries within 2 hours
- Monitor conversion funnel (Mixpanel)
- Collect first testimonials and reviews
- Publish 1 blog post/week
- A/B test registration gate messaging

**Week 3-4: Momentum Building**
- Goal: 100 registrations, 20 print orders
- Share user testimonials and book photos
- Launch first paid ads (Instagram/Facebook, €500 budget)
- Reach out to 10 more influencers
- Optimize based on analytics (drop-off points)

**Month 2-3: Hebrew Market Launch (Israel)**
- Hebrew website version live
- Partner with Israeli parenting influencers (5-10)
- Post in Israeli parenting forums (Tapuz, Mamy)
- Press release to Israeli tech media (Ynet, Calcalist)
- Hebrew-specific ad campaigns
- Goal: 50 Israeli registrations, 10 print orders

**Post-Launch (Month 1-3)**:

**Month 1: Optimize & Iterate**
- Deliver first 20-30 print books
- Collect parent testimonials and book photos
- Refine UX based on user feedback (Mixpanel data)
- A/B test registration gate placement and messaging
- Optimize AI prompts for story quality
- Goal: 500 registrations, 100 print orders, 20 subscriptions

**Month 2: Scale Channels**
- Launch paid ads (Instagram/Facebook, €1,000/month)
- Partner with 10 more influencers
- Publish 4 blog posts (SEO-optimized)
- Launch referral program
- First PR coverage in German parenting media
- Goal: 1,000 registrations, 250 print orders, 50 subscriptions

**Month 3: Expand & Refine**
- Increase ad budget to €2,000/month (if ROAS >3:1)
- Launch Google Ads (€500/month)
- Hebrew market launch (Israel)
- Case studies published (3-5 parent testimonials)
- Seasonal campaign (Easter/Passover)
- Goal: 1,500 registrations, 400 print orders, 80 subscriptions

## Measurement

**North Star Metric**: Monthly Active Book Creators (users who complete a story)

**Input Metrics** (Leading indicators):

**Acquisition Funnel**:
- Website visitors: 1,000-2,000/month (Month 1), 5,000-10,000/month (Month 6)
- Visitor → Free book creation: 15-25%
- Free book creators: 150-500/month (Month 1), 750-2,500/month (Month 6)
- Story completion rate: 60-70% (start → finish story)
- Completed stories: 90-350/month (Month 1), 450-1,750/month (Month 6)
- Registration rate: 25-35% (finish story → register to print)
- Registered users: 23-123/month (Month 1), 113-613/month (Month 6)
- Print conversion: 20-30% (register → order print)
- Print orders: 5-37/month (Month 1), 23-184/month (Month 6)
- Subscription conversion: 10-15% (register → subscribe)

**Channel Performance**:
- Organic (SEO, word-of-mouth): 40% of traffic, €0-5 CAC
- Influencer partnerships: 30% of traffic, €10-20 CAC
- Paid social (Instagram/Facebook): 20% of traffic, €20-40 CAC
- Referrals: 10% of traffic, €0-5 CAC

**Content Performance**:
- Instagram followers: 500 (Month 3), 2,000 (Month 6), 5,000 (Month 12)
- Instagram engagement rate: 3-5%
- Email open rate: >30% (parenting content performs well)
- Email click rate: >8%
- Blog traffic: 500-1,000 visitors/month (Month 3), 2,000-5,000/month (Month 6)

**User Engagement**:
- Time to first book: <15 minutes (target)
- Books per user: 1.2 (Month 3), 1.5 (Month 6), 2.2 (Month 12)
- Repeat purchase rate: 30% (Month 6), 40% (Month 12)
- Sharing rate: 20% share digital book on social media

**Output Metrics** (Lagging indicators):

**Revenue**:
- Monthly Revenue: €2,000 (Month 3), €8,000 (Month 6), €15,000 (Month 12)
- Print revenue: 70-80% of total (Year 1)
- Subscription revenue: 20-30% of total (Year 1)
- Average order value: €35 (print book)
- Customer lifetime value (LTV): €70-120 (2-3 books + potential subscription)

**User Metrics**:
- Total registered users: 100 (Month 3), 500 (Month 6), 2,500 (Month 12)
- Active subscribers: 10 (Month 3), 50 (Month 6), 250 (Month 12)
- Monthly recurring revenue (MRR): €70 (Month 3), €350 (Month 6), €1,750 (Month 12)
- Churn rate: <5% monthly (subscriptions)

**Quality Metrics**:
- Print quality issues: <2% of orders
- Refund rate: <1% of revenue
- Customer support tickets: <5% of orders
- Parent satisfaction (CSAT): ≥4.7/5
- Net Promoter Score (NPS): ≥60

**Operational**:
- Gross margin: 50-60% blended (30-35% print, 90%+ subscriptions)
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC): €15-45
- LTV:CAC ratio: 7:1 - 15:1
- Print fulfillment time: 5-10 days (Germany/Austria), 7-14 days (Israel)

**Review Cadence**:
- Daily: Conversion funnel, print orders, customer support
- Weekly: Channel performance, user engagement, content performance
- Monthly: Revenue, user growth, quality metrics, CAC/LTV
- Quarterly: Strategic review, market expansion, product roadmap

## Guidelines

### ICP Definition
- Segment by demographics (age, income, location), psychographics (values, pain points, tech comfort).
- Identify decision makers (mother 70%, father 20%, grandparents 10%).
- Map buying triggers (birthdays, holidays, milestones, gifting).

### Positioning
- One-sentence positioning: For [parents], [KidBook Creator] is an [AI storybook platform] that [creates personalized books in minutes]; unlike [template services], we [offer unlimited AI creativity with free first book].
- Test positioning with 20-30 beta parents before launch.

### Channel Strategy
- Owned: Build for long-term (website, email, Instagram, blog).
- Earned: Leverage for credibility (influencers, PR, word-of-mouth, communities).
- Paid: Scale what works (start Month 3+, optimize ROAS, scale winners).
- Partnerships: Multiply reach (influencers, print partners, parenting blogs).

### Launch Plan
- Pre-launch: 8 weeks for foundation, beta testing, content creation.
- Launch: Germany/Austria first (Week 0), Israel second (Month 2-3).
- Post-launch: Optimize funnel, scale channels, expand markets.

### Measurement
- North Star Metric: Monthly Active Book Creators (completed stories).
- Input Metrics: Funnel conversion, channel performance, engagement.
- Output Metrics: Revenue, users, LTV, CAC, satisfaction.
- Review Cadence: Daily (funnel), Weekly (channels), Monthly (revenue/growth), Quarterly (strategy).
