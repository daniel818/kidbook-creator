## The Solution: Two-Stage Generation Model

### Recommended Primary Strategy

**Stage 1: Free Preview** ($0.10 cost, 80% reduction)
- ‚úÖ Complete story text (all pages)
- ‚úÖ Character extraction and style definition
- ‚úÖ **1 full-quality hero image** (cover or page 1)
- ‚úÖ **10 blurred placeholder thumbnails** (low-res previews)
- ‚úÖ Full flip-book viewer experience

**Stage 2: Unlock After Payment** ($0.40 cost)
- Generate remaining 10 high-quality illustrations
- Deliver print-ready PDF
- Process physical book order

### Why This Works

**Cost Reduction**:
- Preview: $0.10 (vs $0.50 current)
- **80% cost savings** for non-converters
- Full generation only after payment guarantee

**Conversion Improvement**:
- User sees quality (1 full image) before committing
- Story is complete and readable (emotional investment)
- FOMO created by blurred images ("I want to see the rest!")
- Expected conversion: **15-20%** (vs 3% current) = **5x improvement**

**Effective CAC**:
- Current: $0.50 √∑ 0.03 = $16.67
- New: $0.10 + ($0.40 √ó 0.15) = **$0.16** per user
- **100x improvement in unit economics**

- A/B test multiple preview formats

**Likelihood**: Medium | **Impact**: High

- Show full story text (emotional investment)
- Provide 3D book mockup
- Satisfaction guarantee

### Risk 3: Templates Cannibalize Custom Creation
**Likelihood**: High | **Impact**: Medium

**Mitigation**:
- Price templates lower (‚Ç¨25 vs ‚Ç¨35)

- Promote custom as premium option
