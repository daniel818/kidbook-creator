**Childbook.ai:**

- Photos: maximum 14 days after subscription ends, then deleted
- Illustrations: kept permanently

**MeBook.ai:**
- Collect only when truly needed
- Retain only as long as necessary to provide service
- Protect within commercially acceptable means

**Magical Children's Book:**

**Specific Retention Periods:**
- **Account Data:** As long as account active, or until deletion requested
- **Order and Invoice Data:** Minimum 7 years (statutory tax obligation)

- Legal obligations (7-year tax records)

✓ **Generated books (digital):** 90 days for reorder, then deletion
✓ **Communication data:** 2 years after resolution
✓ **Analytics data:** 26 months (Google Analytics standard)
✓ **Marketing data:** Until consent withdrawn + reasonable period

✓ Automatic deletion processes
