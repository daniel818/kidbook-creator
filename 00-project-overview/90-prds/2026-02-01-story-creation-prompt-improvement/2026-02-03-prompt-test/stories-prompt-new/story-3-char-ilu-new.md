# Story 3 - Character & Illustrations (Improved)

## Character Reference Description
A young toddler boy with dark blonde, curly hair and big blue eyes. He has an expressive face and is wearing a blue and white striped shirt.

## Illustration Prompts
*Style: Modern children's book illustration, vibrant colors, soft lighting*

### Page 1
**Scene:** A cheerful nursery flooded with warm morning sunlight streaming through sheer white curtains. The walls are painted soft buttercup yellow with hand-stenciled clouds. A wooden high chair sits near a window. Plush toys sit on a nearby shelf. He sits in the high chair, arms stretched wide open in an invitation for a hug, face beaming with pure joy. Medium shot, warm and inviting.

### Page 2
**Scene:** A cozy corner with a soft cream-colored shag rug underfoot. Sunlight pools on the carpet. Building blocks in primary colors are scattered nearby. A beloved fluffy brown teddy bear with a red ribbon fills part of the frame. He hugs the teddy tightly to his chest, chin resting on its fuzzy head, eyes closed in contentment. Close-up, tender and loving.

### Page 3
**Scene:** An extreme close-up capturing the soft texture of teddy bear fur against a child's cheek. In the background, the yellow nursery walls glow warmly. Soft window light illuminates the scene. His face presses against the teddy, eyes closed, a blissful smile spreading across his features. Intimate macro shot.

### Page 4
**Scene:** A bright living room with hardwood floors polished to a honey shine. A comfortable beige sofa sits against pale blue walls. Family photos hang above. Mommy stands nearby in a soft lavender sweater. He reaches up with both arms extended high, bouncing on his toes with excitement, face lit with surprised delight. Medium shot.

Close two-shot, warmth and security.
